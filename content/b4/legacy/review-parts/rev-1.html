Exercise 3 Find and Correct the Mistake

Each sentence has ONE mistake with the Present Simple or Simple Past. Rewrite the sentence correctly on the line.

1.

Correct: She works at a hospital every day.
2.

Correct: He doesn't like cold weather.
3.

Correct: Do they speak English at home?
4.

5.
