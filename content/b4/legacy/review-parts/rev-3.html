Exercise 4 Find and Correct the Mistake

Each sentence has ONE mistake (wrong participle, missing have/has, wrong tense, or since/for). Rewrite the sentence correctly on the line.

1.

I have went to the supermarket already.

Correct: I have gone to the supermarket already.
2.

She written three emails today.

Correct: She has written three emails today.
3.

We have studied English since three years.

Correct: We have studied English for three years.
4.

They have visited Paris last year.

Correct: They visited Paris last year.
5.

He has eat lunch already.

Correct: He has eaten lunch already.
6.

Have you finish your homework yet?

Correct: Have you finished your homework yet?
