Exercise 4 Rewrite in the Passive Voice

Rewrite each active sentence in the passive voice. Keep the same tense. Include the agent with by only when it is important.

1.

A Brazilian company makes these shoes. (Present Simple)

Correct: These shoes are made by a Brazilian company.
2.

The Romans built this road. (Past Simple)

Correct: This road was built by the Romans.
3.

Someone has stolen my wallet. (Present Perfect)

Correct: My wallet has been stolen.
4.

The mayor will open the festival on Friday. (Future)

Correct: The festival will be opened by the mayor on Friday.
5.

Workers are cleaning the streets right now. (Present Continuous)

Correct: The streets are being cleaned right now.
6.

You must complete the form in black ink. (Modal)

Correct: The form must be completed in black ink.
