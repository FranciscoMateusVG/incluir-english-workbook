Exercise 4 Find and correct the mistake

Each sentence has ONE error in the future form, modal verb, or preposition. Rewrite the sentence correctly on the line.

1.

She can to swim very well.

Correct: She can swim very well.
2.

He must studies for the exam.

Correct: He must study for the exam.
3.

We have English class in Wednesdays.

Correct: We have English class on Wednesdays.
4.

I like to read in night.

Correct: I like to read at night.
5.

Look at the time! We will be late. (rewrite using going to)

Correct: Look at the time! We are going to be late.
6.

My brother is at home, but my parents are at London.

Correct: My brother is at home, but my parents are in London.
