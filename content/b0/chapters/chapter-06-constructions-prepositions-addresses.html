Chapter 6

Basic Constructions, Prepositions of Place, Addresses

In this chapter you will learn how to make simple sentences in English (como fazer frases simples em ingles), how to say where things are (como dizer onde as coisas estao), and how to talk about places in your city (como falar sobre lugares na sua cidade).

Unit 6.1

Basic Sentence Constructions

📐 Simple Sentence Patterns (Padroes de Frases Simples)

In English, we use simple patterns to make sentences. Here are the most important ones for beginners:

Em ingles, usamos padroes simples para fazer frases. Aqui estao os mais importantes para iniciantes:

English Pattern Portugues Example (Exemplo)
This is a ___. Isto e um/uma ___. This is a book. (Isto e um livro.)
I have a ___. Eu tenho um/uma ___. I have a pen. (Eu tenho uma caneta.)
I like ___. Eu gosto de ___. I like music. (Eu gosto de musica.)
I want ___. Eu quero ___. I want water. (Eu quero agua.)
There is a ___. Ha um/uma ___. There is a cat. (Ha um gato.)
There are ___. Ha ___ (plural). There are two dogs. (Ha dois cachorros.)

Examples (Exemplos)

This is...

This is a pencil.

Isto e um lapis.

This is...

Isto e uma escola.

I have...

I have a notebook.

I like...

I like soccer.

Eu gosto de futebol.

I want...

I want a sandwich.

There is...

💡
Tip (Dica):

"This is" vs "These are" — singular vs plural:

  • This is a book. = Isto e um livro. (1 livro = singular)
  • These are books. = Estes/Estas sao livros. (2 ou mais = plural)

The same idea applies with "There is" (singular) and "There are" (plural):

  • There is a pen. = Ha uma caneta. (1 caneta)
  • There are two pens. = Ha duas canetas. (2 canetas)
Teacher Note:

Suggested time: 20 minutes

Warm-up: Bring everyday objects (pen, book, eraser, water bottle) and hold them up one at a time. Say "This is a ___" for each one and have students repeat. Then have students hold up their own objects and say "I have a ___."

L1 support: At B0 level, students may need every instruction repeated in Portuguese. Encourage them but do not rush. The goal is recognition and basic production, not fluency.

Common issue: Students may say "Have a book" without "I". Remind them that English always needs a subject: "I have", "You have", etc.

Unit 6.2

Prepositions of Place (Preposicoes de Lugar)

📐 Prepositions of Place (Preposicoes de Lugar)

Prepositions of place tell us where something is. (Preposicoes de lugar nos dizem onde algo esta.)

English Portugues Example (Exemplo)
in em, dentro de The book is in the bag. (O livro esta dentro da bolsa.)
on em, sobre The pen is on the desk. (A caneta esta sobre a mesa.)
under embaixo de The cat is under the table. (O gato esta embaixo da mesa.)
next to ao lado de The chair is next to the door. (A cadeira esta ao lado da porta.)
between entre The school is between the park and the library. (A escola fica entre o parque e a biblioteca.)
in front of na frente de The car is in front of the house. (O carro esta na frente da casa.)
behind atras de The garden is behind the house. (O jardim fica atras da casa.)

Vocabulary: Prepositions (Vocabulario: Preposicoes)

English Portugues Example Sentence (Frase de Exemplo)
in em, dentro de The water is in the bottle. (A agua esta na garrafa.)
on em, sobre The phone is on the table. (O celular esta sobre a mesa.)
under embaixo de The shoes are under the bed. (Os sapatos estao embaixo da cama.)
next to ao lado de Ana is next to Bruno. (Ana esta ao lado de Bruno.)
between entre The teacher is between the two desks. (A professora esta entre as duas mesas.)
in front of na frente de The bus is in front of the school. (O onibus esta na frente da escola.)
behind atras de The ball is behind the door. (A bola esta atras da porta.)

Examples in Context (Exemplos em Contexto)

in

My pencil is in my backpack.

Meu lapis esta dentro da minha mochila.

on

The notebook is on the desk.

O caderno esta sobre a carteira.

under

The dog is under the chair.

O cachorro esta embaixo da cadeira.

next to

The pharmacy is next to the supermarket.

A farmacia fica ao lado do supermercado.

between

Lucas is between Maria and Pedro.

Lucas esta entre Maria e Pedro.

in front of

The teacher is in front of the class.

A professora esta na frente da turma.

behind

The tree is behind the house.

A arvore fica atras da casa.

📌
Remember (Lembre-se):
  • in = inside containers, rooms, bags, boxes (dentro de recipientes, salas, bolsas, caixas)
  • on = on top of surfaces like tables, desks, walls (em cima de superficies como mesas, carteiras, paredes)
  • under = below something (embaixo de algo)

These three are the most common! (Essas tres sao as mais comuns!)

Teacher Note:

Suggested time: 25 minutes

Activity: Use a box and a small toy (or eraser). Place the toy IN the box, ON the box, UNDER the box, etc. Each time, ask: "Where is the [toy]?" and model the answer: "It is in/on/under the box." Have students take turns placing the object and saying the sentence.

TPR (Total Physical Response): Say a preposition and have students physically show it using their hands and desk. For example: "Put your eraser ON the desk. Put your eraser IN your backpack."

📐 Asking and Saying Where Things Are (Perguntar e dizer onde as coisas estao)

Use these patterns to ask about locations and give answers:

English Pattern Portugues

Dialogue: Asking for Directions (Dialogo: Pedindo informacoes)

Leticia is new in the neighborhood. She asks Rafael about places. (Leticia e nova no bairro. Ela pergunta ao Rafael sobre os lugares.)
Leticia: Hi, Rafael! Where is the school?
Rafael: Hi, Leticia! The school is on Rua das Palmeiras. It is next to the park.
Leticia: And where is the supermarket?
Rafael: The supermarket is on Avenida Brasil. It is between the bank and the pharmacy.
Leticia: Is there a library in the neighborhood?
Rafael: Yes! There is a library behind the school.
Leticia: Where is the bus stop?
Rafael: The bus stop is in front of the church, on Rua das Palmeiras.
Leticia: Thank you, Rafael!
Rafael: You're welcome!

Traducao do dialogo:

Leticia: Oi, Rafael! Onde fica a escola? / Rafael: Oi, Leticia! A escola fica na Rua das Palmeiras. Fica ao lado do parque. / Leticia: E onde fica o supermercado? / Rafael: O supermercado fica na Avenida Brasil. Fica entre o banco e a farmacia. / Leticia: Tem uma biblioteca no bairro? / Rafael: Sim! Tem uma biblioteca atras da escola. / Leticia: Onde fica o ponto de onibus? / Rafael: O ponto de onibus fica na frente da igreja, na Rua das Palmeiras. / Leticia: Obrigada, Rafael! / Rafael: De nada!

Teacher Note:

Suggested time: 25 minutes

Activity: Have students read the dialogue in pairs. Then draw a simple map on the board and ask students to place the buildings in the correct locations based on the dialogue. This helps them connect the language to spatial understanding.

Extension: Ask students about their own neighborhood: "Where is the school?" "Where is the supermarket?" Accept answers in simple patterns even if not perfectly grammatical.

Unit 6.4

Reading & Writing (Leitura e Escrita)

Reading: My Neighborhood (Leitura: Meu Bairro)

Read the text about Fernanda's neighborhood. (Leia o texto sobre o bairro da Fernanda.)

My name is Fernanda. I live in Belo Horizonte. My house is on Rua Sao Jose. It is a small street.

There is a school next to my house. The school is called Escola Incluir. There is a park behind the school. I like the park. There are many trees in the park.

The supermarket is on Avenida Brasil. It is between the bank and the pharmacy. I want to go to the supermarket today.

There is a church in front of the park. The bus stop is next to the church. I have a friend who lives on the same street. Her name is Julia. Julia's house is behind the church.

Traducao do texto: Meu nome e Fernanda. Eu moro em Belo Horizonte. Minha casa fica na Rua Sao Jose. E uma rua pequena. / Tem uma escola ao lado da minha casa. A escola se chama Escola Incluir. Tem um parque atras da escola. Eu gosto do parque. Ha muitas arvores no parque. / O supermercado fica na Avenida Brasil. Fica entre o banco e a farmacia. Eu quero ir ao supermercado hoje. / Tem uma igreja na frente do parque. O ponto de onibus fica ao lado da igreja. Eu tenho uma amiga que mora na mesma rua. O nome dela e Julia. A casa da Julia fica atras da igreja.

Teacher Note:

Suggested time: 25 minutes (reading + exercises 7-8)

Pre-reading: Ask students in Portuguese what places are near their house. Write a few on the board in English to activate vocabulary from Unit 6.3.

Reading strategy: Read the text aloud slowly. Then have students read silently. For B0 students, you may read each sentence and ask: "What does this mean?" to check comprehension before moving to the exercises.

Drawing activity: Have students draw a simple map of Fernanda's neighborhood based on the text. This reinforces spatial vocabulary without requiring writing.
