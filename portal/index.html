Level Map

Four levels, one direction

B1

Personal and classroom survival

Students learn to talk about themselves and function in class.

B2

Daily life survival

Students handle places, food, shopping, directions, and now-actions.

B3

Stories, plans, advice

Students explain what happened, what comes next, and what to do.

B4

Survival English capstone

Students solve realistic problems and prepare for the speaking mission.
