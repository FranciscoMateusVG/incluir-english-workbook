Course map

Four levels, one practical progression

Each level gives students a larger world in which they can act with English.

B1

Start here

Personal and classroom survival

Talk about yourself and function in class.

B2

Daily life

Places, food, and things happening now

Handle basic daily interactions with confidence.

B3

Build range

Stories, plans, advice, and problems

Explain events and make simple decisions.

B4

Capstone

Survival English in real situations

Solve predictable problems and keep talking.
